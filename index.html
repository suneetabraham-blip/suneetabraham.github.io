Featured projects

Pricing · Causal · Production

Shapley-Value Price-Gap Decomposition

The existing price-gap method was limited: 75% of data fell into "both prices changed" and was uninformative. I built a Shapley distribution that decomposes the gap into four components (Walmart's contribution, the rest of the market's, a mixed contribution from units sold, and assortment), pivotable by department, state, region, and UPC.

Merchants use it to see whether Walmart is leading or following market price moves in each segment, understand what drove each move, and identify where to push back with CPG suppliers when the data supports it. Rolled out across 7 Walmart International markets and 1M+ UPCs.

Impact Live across 7 markets · Segment-level leader vs follower view · Drives CPG negotiations
GenAI · Gemini · BigQuery · Pricing

Gemini + BigQuery AI Agent for Pricing Analytics

Pricing answers lived across 12 separate dashboards. Merchants had to know which one to open and what filter to apply, and the analytics team became the bottleneck for anything cross-cutting. I built a Gemini-powered agent over BigQuery, grounded in the team's dashboard SQL and metric definitions, so the answer comes back in the same language the dashboards use.

One plain-English interface replaces 12 dashboard hunts. Merchants self-serve; the analytics team focuses on the cross-cutting work that actually needed a human.

Impact 12 dashboards into one Q&A interface · Merchant self-serve · Grounded in team SQL + metric definitions
Data Quality · Tooling · VBA

Dev-vs-Production Dashboard QC Tool

Validating a dashboard release used to mean an analyst manually diffing dev vs production for hours. I built an Excel + VBA tool that takes the chore off the queue: paste both datasets into one sheet sharing only the first column as a key, and the macro auto-detects matching schema across the rest of the columns, computes overall match rate, and flags every cell-level divergence.

Zero configuration from the user. Anchoring on a single shared column and letting the macro do the rest is what made it actually get used by the team.

Impact Release QC became self-serve · Auto-discovers matching schema · Cell-level diff output
Pricing · Experimentation

Global Pricing Rebuild with Purchasing Power Parity

Prices hadn't been updated in two years. I researched YouTube pricing benchmarks, purchase volumes, user volumes, and PPP across markets. Clustered countries into four segments and anchored prices on the best-performing country in each cluster. Staged rollout via A/B tests.

Impact 37% revenue lift · 27% subscription growth
